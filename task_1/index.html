ABOUT US

We are a team of passionate cultivators and sellers with decades of experience in growing and delivering premium-quality mangoes. We understand what makes a mango truly exceptional from its aroma and ripeness to its texture and taste. This tropical delight thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained, alluvial or loamy soil rich in organic content. Fortunately, we are based in India, the land revered as the world's largest and most celebrated producer of mangoes often referred to as the King of Fruits. Our mangoes are nurtured in regions where nature offers the perfect harmony of sunlight, soil, and seasonal rhythm. These factors make Indian mangoes especially varieties like Alphonso, Kesar, and Dasheri, stand out globally for their rich flavor, vibrant color, velvety pulp, and unmatched sweetness. At our core, we bring together traditional farming wisdom with modern standards to deliver mangoes that are not only 100% organic but also a true taste of heritage and tropical bliss.

VARIETIES

Alphonso
Known as the “King of Mangoes,” Alphonso is rich, creamy, and juicy with a delightful aroma. It ripens between April and June.
Kesar
Famous for its bright saffron color and sweet aroma, Kesar mangoes are cultivated in Gujarat and harvested from May to July.
Dasheri
A traditional North Indian variety, Dasheri is long, yellow, and very sweet. It ripens around mid-June and is juicy and aromatic.
Langra
Originating from Varanasi, Langra mangoes are fibrous, flavorful, and tangy. They ripen in July and are popular in eastern India.
Himsagar
Native to Bengal, Himsagar mangoes are juicy, fiberless, and extremely sweet. They have a short season from May to early June.
Banganapalli
A popular mango from Andhra Pradesh, Banganapalli is large, golden-yellow, and has firm, fiberless flesh. It ripens in May.
